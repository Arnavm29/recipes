Chicken Confit

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 skin-on, bone-in whole chicken legs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, halved
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 4 to 5 cups olive oil

Method

  1. Prepare the chicken:
    • Place chicken into a large baking dish
    • Tuck garlic, thyme, cloves, and bay leaves around chicken
    • Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 24 hours
  2. Preheat oven to 250F
  3. Cook the chicken:
    • Remove chicken from baking dish
    • Place chicken in dutch oven, cover with olive oil
    • Start pan on medium heat, wait till oil heats up
    • Cover pan with foil and place in oven
  4. Cool the chicken:
    • Remove from pan and let cool for 30 minutes
    • Transfer chicken to platter
  5. Store the chicken:
    • Transfer to oil
    • Cover with foil
  6. Heat the chicken:
    • Add chicken to stovetop
    • Cook until skin is golden brown
    • Flip and cook on both sides
  7. Serve with roasted potatoes or green salad
