Chicken Confit

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 skin-on, bone-in whole chicken legs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, halved
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 4 to 5 cups olive oil

Method

  1. Prepare the chicken:
    • Place chicken into a large baking dish
    • Tuck garlic, thyme, cloves, and bay leaves around chicken
    • Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 24 hours
  2. Preheat oven to 250F
  3. Cook the chicken
    • Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat, add little oil or butter
    • Drop spoonfuls of pancake batter into pan
    • Cook until both sides are pancake brown
  4. Flip:
    • Flip pancakes gently with spatula
    • Cook until boths sides are golden brown
  5. Serve:
    • Eat immediatly
    • Store leftover pancake in fridge
